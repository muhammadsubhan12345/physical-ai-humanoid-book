## Humans and Robots Together

The future of work is not humans versus robots.

It is humans **working alongside** intelligent machines.

Physical AI enables robots to assist rather than replace humans.

---

## New Skills and Roles

As Physical AI grows, new roles will emerge:
- Robot supervisors
- AI trainers
- Safety engineers
- Human-robot interaction designers

Education must adapt to these changes.

---

## Ethical and Safety Considerations

Physical AI systems must be:
- Safe
- Transparent
- Aligned with human values

Poorly designed systems can cause harm.

---

## Looking Ahead

Humanoid robots will:
- Enter workplaces
- Assist in daily tasks
- Improve productivity

Understanding Physical AI is essential for the future.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Physical AI will reshape work
- Humans and robots will collaborate
- Education must prepare for this future
