## What Are Humanoid Robots?

Humanoid robots are machines designed to **resemble the human body** in structure and movement.

They typically include:
- Head
- Torso
- Arms
- Legs

This design allows them to operate in environments built for humans.

---

## Why Build Humanoid Robots?

Humanoid robots are useful because:
- Human spaces are designed for human bodies
- They can use existing tools
- They can assist humans directly

They are especially useful in healthcare, manufacturing, and service industries.

## Challenges in Humanoid Robotics

Building humanoid robots is difficult due to:
- Balance and locomotion
- Energy efficiency
- Real-time control
- Safety around humans

Each challenge requires intelligent decision-making.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Humanoid robots mimic human form
- They operate in human environments
- Physical AI enables their intelligence
- Safety and control are critical
