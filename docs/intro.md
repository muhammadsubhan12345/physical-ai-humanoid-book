Welcome to **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**, a foundational course designed to prepare learners for the future where **humans, intelligent agents, and robots work together**.

---

## Why Physical AI Matters

Most AI systems today live inside screens.  
Physical AI brings intelligence **into the physical world**.

Examples include:
- Humanoid robots
- Autonomous vehicles
- Smart manufacturing robots
- Assistive healthcare robots

As industries adopt these systems, there is a growing demand for engineers and thinkers who understand **both AI and physical systems**.
